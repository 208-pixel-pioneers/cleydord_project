About Us

Welcome to Claydord Appliances, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2005 by Daniel Brown, Claydord Appliances has come a long way from its beginnings in a Ghana. When Daniel Brown first started out, his passion for helping other parents be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for his fellow sports lover drove him to do intense research, quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the Accra, the world, the Austin area, and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the sports industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
